Target Audience:

The target audience of weathersight.com web site is described below:

-Who: Professionals, parents, teenagers, outdoorsman, sportsmen, and athletes

-Age: 16 to 100

-Motivation: Improve knowledge about the weather in their area.

Personas:

Name: Emily Adams
Occupation: Event Manager
Demographics and Education: 35 years old. Has a Bachelor’s degree in Marketing.
Goals and motivation for using the site: Learn what the weather will be like on a specific day in order to plan her outdoor event accordingly.
Social: Very busy individual. Regularly travels to other locations. Has two children that are very active and love to play at the park.
Technology: Tech savvy individual who uses the latest PC technology and android devices.

Name: Daniel Villalobos
Occupation: Landscaper
Demographics and Education: 28 years old. Has a Master’s Degree from MIT in Bio-Physics.
Goals and motivation for using the site: To learn what the weather will be so he can dress accordingly and bring the proper tools with him to work.
Social: Burnt out from school and wants to take a break doing the one thing that makes him happy. Working with his hands and landscaping. Loves to interact with new people and his new job gives him the opportunity.
Technology: Loves technology but has distanced himself. He uses a basic flip phone and currently uses his old school MacBook to check things out on the Internet.

Scenarios:

1. How will the weather affect my outdoor wedding reception?
2. How will the weather affect my photo shoot?
3. Will I need to wear a parka to the soccer game?
4. Will it be too hot to wear a fuzzy sweater and Ugg’s?
5. How should I pack my children’s clothing for our upcoming vacation?
