Fish Haven, Id

Fish Haven, Idaho is an undiscovered paradise. The first settlement at Fish Haven was made in 1864. A post office called Fish Haven was established in 1867 and remained in operation until 1962. The community was so named because nearby Bear Lake is a favorite fishing spot

Most people who drive by recognize the raw beauty of a place ringed by high desert mountains and bordered by the shores of a vast natural lake. In winter the hills outside this small paradise echo with the sharp whine of snow machines and in the summer thousands of tourists ply the lake's bright blue waters with water skis or fishing poles. But they don't live there. It's different living in Fish Haven.
